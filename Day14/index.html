In Chinese culture, lilies are known to symbolize good luck and long lasting love. In addition to their usage at religious ceremonies, lilies have also been used for medicinal purposes. The buds or oils of the lily have been known to reduce fever and make skin more firm, thus lessening wrinkles.
No wonder roses have been referenced in literature and music for centuries. Archaeologists have discovered rose fossils dating back 35 million years. An even more amazing fact about roses is that the oldest living rose is 1,000 years old.
